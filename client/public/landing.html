Features

Everything you need to stream and connect

A complete communication platform built for privacy, performance, and flexibility.

RTMP Live Streaming

Stream directly from OBS, Streamlabs, or any RTMP-compatible software. Low-latency HLS delivery ensures your audience stays engaged.

WebRTC Voice Chat

Crystal-clear voice communication powered by WebRTC. Join voice channels with push-to-talk or voice activation.

Real-Time Chat

Instant messaging with Socket.IO. Create text channels, share messages, and stay connected with your community.

Privacy First

Your data stays on your server. No tracking, no ads, no third-party access. Complete control over your content.

Cross-Platform

Access from anywhere. Native apps for Windows, macOS, Linux, Android, and iOS. Or use the web app directly.

Fully Customizable

Open source and extensible. Modify the code, add features, or integrate with your existing infrastructure.

Deploy in minutes,
own it forever

Datasetto runs on your hardware. A simple Docker setup means you can have your own streaming platform running in minutes, whether on a Raspberry Pi, VPS, or enterprise server.

  • Full control over your data and content
  • No monthly fees or usage limits
  • Automatic HTTPS with Caddy
  • Redis-backed persistent storage
# Clone the repository
git clone https://github.com/FilippoLeone/datasetto

# Navigate to ops directory
cd datasetto/ops

# Configure environment
cp .env.example .env

# Launch with Docker
docker compose -f docker-compose.prod.yml up -d
